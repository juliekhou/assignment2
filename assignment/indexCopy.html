2023 Simplified Tax Form

Income
1 Wages, salaries, and tips. This should be shown in box 1 of your Form(s) W-2. Attach your Form(s) W-2.
2 Taxable interest from 1099 forms
3 Other Income not included in 1 and 2.
4 Add lines 1, 2, and 3. This is your adjusted gross income.
5 If this is a join return, add your spouse as a dependent. Write $13,850 for an individual return and $27,700 for a joint return.
Spouse
6 Subtract line 5 from line 4. If line 5 is larger than line 4, enter -0-. This is your taxable income.
Payments, Credits, and Tax
7 Federal income tax withheld from Form(s) W-2 and 1099.
8 Earned income credit (EIC)
9 Add lines 7 and 8a. These are your total payments and credits.
10 Tax. Use the amount on line 6 above to find your tax in the tax table in the instructions. Then, enter the tax from the table on
this line.
11 Other Taxes
12 Add lines 10 and 11. This is your total tax.
Refund
13 If line 9 is larger than line 12, subtract line 12 from line 9. This is your refund. If form 8888 is attached, check here
Amount You Owe
14 If line 12 is larger than line 9, subtract line 9 from line 12. This is the amount you owe. For details on how to pay, see
instructions.
